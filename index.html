    Our Classes

    Here at Sacred Souls Yoga, we want our students to have the most wholesome experience of yoga possible! 

    To put it simply- Our goal is to teach students how to be in the present moment; how to connect with their bodies, mind, and emotions.

    Once that begins to happen, people start to see all kinds of other positive changes in their lives. We know that just by moving your body with simple awareness you can open up the doors to your own emotional, mental, physical and spiritual well-being, and healing. 

    Founder & Instructor
    Komal Majmundar

     

    Yoga was present in my life from a young age. I remember as a child there were yoga books around the house, and sometimes I would look through them and try poses. But my curiosity was not much more than that until college, when I started struggling with anxiety and depression. It seemed to come out of nowhere, and I couldn’t quite figure out what the cause of it was. On the outside, things were all functioning fine in my life. But on the inside I felt deeply disconnected within myself and with the world around me, and didn’t know what to do about it. I tried everything I could think of, and nothing helped. Then one day a friend introduced me to yoga, and I was drawn to it immediately! I was amazed that something like this existed and could actually help me. 

     

    After some time practicing on my own, I understood that I needed a teacher. I decided to get my certification and completed a Teacher Training course at Atmananda Yoga in Manhattan, where I taught classes before I decided to open my own studio. Now, after teaching for almost 6 years, and through my own journey and practice I have realized that people come to yoga for all sorts of reasons, and with different goals in mind. And with minor variations in teaching style I could help them achieve just that! The only thing that is non-negotiable when it comes to yoga is the awareness and mindfulness aspect.
